Pamekasan foods Kota Gerbang Salam

If you go to this city, this is the food that I recommend for you

Flys Satay

It is not literally a flys satay, it is because the meat is cut to a small pieces as small as a fly. It usually ate with lontong and peanut sauce.

Campur Lorjhuk

Campur in english is mix. It is named because we mix many ingridient in it. Lorjhuk is bamboo shells. The ingridient tht used is lontong, bihun noodle, and sprouts
