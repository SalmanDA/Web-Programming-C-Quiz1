Location

Pamekasan is a name of city in east java Indonesia, to be precies, it is in Madura Island. It is located beetween Sampang and Sumenep city. Then, to come here you must be trough Bangkalan and Sampang city from Surabaya, but now you can take a flight to Sumenep city instead, it is far more efficient. This city is bordered by the Java sea in the north, the Madura Strait in the south, Sampang in the west, and Sumenep in the east.

Tradition

Actually, karapan sapi is Madura tradition, in Pamekasan we often hold this tradition and so much people joining to watch it. Karapan sapi is a cow race that someone rides. There are many farmer joining this competion, even a 5 grade elementary school also ever take a part. The purpose of karapan sapi is to looking for the fastest couple of cowa that are participated in the race.

Traditional Clothes

Sakera is a white-red clothe and black outer plus a hat named sode' that Madurese people wear during wartime.
