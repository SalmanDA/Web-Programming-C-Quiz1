Pamekasan Tourist Site Kota Gerbang Salam

If you go to this city, this is places that I recommend for you

Arek Lancor

This monument is the center of Pamekasan city. Arek is celurit in indonesian, but it is in different shape like it is worn out. That's why it is named arek lancor, in Madura language it means broken clurit.

Jhumiang Beach

This a beach that located in south Pamekasan, Pademawu village. It is a beach with many photo spot that will remind you to this place and make you feel missing this place. People often come here enjoy sunset, sunrise and the ocean breeze.
